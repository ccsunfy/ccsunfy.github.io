Biography

I am Fangyu Sun (孙方宇-in Chinese), currently a direct Ph.D. student in Information and Communication Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests include reinforcement learning, differentiable simulation, and end-to-end robotic control. I am advised by Prof. Danping Zou, and I am a member of the ViSYS Lab at SJTU.

In the past three years, I have authored or co-authored seven papers, including four journal and conference papers in venues such as IEEE RAL and IEEE ICRA. I have one accepted national invention patent application, with two first-author papers currently under review.
